Bangladesh Earned Leave

Resolve the manual process for keeping, maintaining the Employees Earn leaves.

by Metamorphosis Ltd.

Overview

Our Company credits 21 days' of Earned Leave to an employee after the completion of one year from the date to joining. The calculation is that for every 18 days' worked, the employee earns one day Earned Leave.

Features

  • Flexibility configurations Employee Earn Leaves Days

  • Calculate Earn leave of Employee according to his/her joining date.
